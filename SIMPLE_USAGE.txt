SIMPLE FAME CHAT INTERFACE
==========================

Just double-click: talk_to_fame_simple.bat

OR

Run: python fame_simple.py

That's it. No GUI, no complexity, just type and talk.

Commands:
- Type normally to chat with FAME
- "list questions" - see unanswered questions
- "answer question 22" - FAME answers question 22
- "answer all" - FAME answers all questions
- "exit" - quit

Simple and works.
